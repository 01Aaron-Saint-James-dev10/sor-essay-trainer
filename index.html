The exam, explained

  • About 3 hours, 3 sections (A, B, C), 9 questions - you answer 3 (one per section).
  • Section A - a religious tradition and one of its variants (a "different flavour" within it).
  • Section B - a contemporary issue (suffering or ethics) and how the tradition responds.
  • Section C - a depth-study topic you've gone deeper on in class.
  • Marked on five things: clear communication, analysing beliefs/values/practices, the variant, the issue, and weighing different points of view.
